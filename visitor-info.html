Frequently Asked Questions

Currency

Taniti uses the U.S. Dollar. Some businesses accept credit cards, but travelers should carry cash for buses and small vendors.

Power

Power outlets are 120v, making travel easier for North American visitors.

Drinking Age

The drinking age is 18.

Health

Hospitals and clinics are available for visitors.

Language

English is spoken in tourist areas. Rural areas may have a stronger language barrier.

Closures

Holiday closures may affect attractions, restaurants, and tour schedules.
