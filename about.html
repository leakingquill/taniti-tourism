Eat in Taniti

Taniti offers local seafood, American-style meals, and international dining options for visiting families.

Local Seafood

Try fresh fish and island-inspired dishes near Yellow Leaf Bay and Merriton Landing.

Family Dining

Casual restaurants offer familiar meals for children and first-time visitors.

Traveler Tip

Most tourist-area restaurants accept U.S. Dollars and major credit cards.
