Transport & Safety

Arrival

Visitors can arrive through the international airport or cruise ship terminal.

Getting Around

Use hotel shuttles, public buses, taxis, walking routes in Taniti City, and bicycle rentals.

Bus Payment

Carry U.S. Dollars for small fares. Credit-card acceptance may vary, so confirm before boarding.

Bike Safety

Wear helmets when biking and follow local rental rules.

Travel Awareness

Stay alert for pickpocketing in crowded tourist areas and keep valuables secure.

Quick Facts

Currency: U.S. Dollar

Power: 120v

Language: English in tourist areas; translation app recommended for rural areas.
